In that time, I've thought long and hard about the drawing for custom tooling ... and decided it would be better if the selection was juried rather than a simple ticket pull. Think about it; everyone would be disappointed if we selected your name and you had something in mind that I couldn't make.

On that note, you probably want to know how the entries will be judged. These are my criteria:

  1. I think it would be fun to make
  2. It would help other glass artists
  3. It's the coolest innovation I've seen recently
  4. It's a tool that multiple artists have asked for
